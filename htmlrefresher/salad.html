Description

This delicious lasagna is made with layers of pasta, cheese, and rich meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and meat sauce.
  4. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes.
